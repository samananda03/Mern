Odisha: India's Best Kept Secret

Tourist Attractions in Odisha

  • Jagannath Temple, Puri- One of Char Dham piligrimage sites and famous for its annual Rath Yatra
  • Konark Sun Temple- UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Chilika Lake- Largest coastal lagoon in India
  • Satkosia Tiger Reserve-Rich biodiversity hotspot home to tigers, elephants and more.
  • Khandagiri Caves- Ancient rock-cut caves with Jain heritage
  • Nandankanan Zoological Park-Unique zoo known for its white tigers.
